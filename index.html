Socheema Int'l School was established in 1995 starting with Socheema Int'l Nursery and Primary School, Lagos, Nigeria. Our Aim at Socheema Int'l School is to be widely acclaimed locally and internationally as a school that trains and turns out students who have been adequately prepared for tertiary education and life.

Our Mission Statement is “To produce the total child through a holistic education programme that benchmarks best practices while being socially responsible.” The school is run strictly on sound management principles with clearly defined structures, systems and highly experienced teachers, staff and Board of Advisors.

About Our School

...........

Socheema Int'l School is an independent, non-partisan, day and boarding school for boys and girls from 18months to 18years. We encourage students to be lifelong learners by instilling in them great confidence, respect and a consciousness of the needs of society.

Socheema Int'l School began in 1985 in the heart of Anthony Village, Lagos. Today, we have established another state of the art campus on the Lekki Peninsula in Lagos. The Socheema Community places great value on character and moral education as well on academics achievements. Our unique education ensures that boys and girls learn in the words of our school song.
